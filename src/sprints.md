1. Influencer Network Graph (Knowledge graph) (Ahrefs: Backlink Profile)

A visual graph using canvas element that shows nodes (curators) which brands they're connected etc.

2. Influencer Network Graph (Ahrefs: Backlink Profile)

For any influencer, show:

- All brands they've promoted
- Relationship history timeline
- Overlap with other influencers (shared brand partnerships)
- Rate card estimation based on engagement

Killer insight: "These 5 influencers all work with your competitor but not you"

3. Brand Explorer (Ahrefs: Site Explorer)

The most requested feature. Instead of searching keywords, search a brand name and see:

- All sponsored content they've paid for
- Which influencers they work with (and their followers/engagement)
- Which keywords/hashtags they target
- Estimated spend (based on influencer rates)

Implementation: Reverse lookup - scrape brand's @mentions, hashtags, and build a profile.

4. Keyword Intelligence (Ahrefs: Keywords Explorer)

For any search term, show:

- Competition level: How many brands are bidding on this term?
- Top spenders: Who dominates this keyword?
- Related keywords: Other terms these brands also target
- Trend data: Is this keyword heating up or cooling? 4. Rank Tracker (Ahrefs: Rank Tracker)

  Track your brand's position for specific keywords over time:
  - Daily/weekly position snapshots
  - Alerts when competitors overtake you
  - Historical position charts

Example: "Alert: CapCut now ranks #1 for 'auto captions' - you dropped to #4"
